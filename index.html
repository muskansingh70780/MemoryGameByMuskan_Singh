SIMPLE MEMORY GAME BY MUSKAN SINGH

Rules:

  • You will start the game by flipping over one card
  • If the next card you flip matches with the first card, then a pop up alert notifies you and you get '+1' to your score
  • These cards will then disappear since it's a match
  • If the next card you flip does not match, then a pop up alert notifies you of this and the cards flip back
  • The game continues until you match all the cards on the board
